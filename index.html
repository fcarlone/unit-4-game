Crystal Collector Game

Instructions:

A random number is generated at the beginning of the game.


You are given four crystals. Each crystal is assigned an unique value.


The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it.


Your mission is to keep adding to your score by clicking on the crystals.


You win by matching the Random Number. You loss if your score is greater than the Random Number.
